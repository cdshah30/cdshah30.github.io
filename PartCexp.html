Rules to Play The Game

First, the player selects the number of different dinausor pictures in the game, and the number of seconds the board will be visible before the tiles are hidden.

Then, the player clicks on tiles in hopes of uncovering dinausor pairs. Pairs will remain visible, tiles that do not match will be hidden again.

The player has a time limit that depends on the size of the game.
